TFL data visualisation

Commute travel times data visualisation

A visualisation of 3 months worth of journey data, illustrating the duration of journey vs. time of day on a 24hr clock.

Visualising data in this way unveils patterns in my travel data: it's apparent that I would usually catch one of two particular trains in the morning, and that the duration of that journey was typically about 25 minutes.

Built using the D3.js (Data Driven Documents) library.
